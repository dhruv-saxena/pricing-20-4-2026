Team seats — 10+ input variations

With just three seat chips (3 / 5 / 10+), the 10+ slot needs a way for users to tell us how many seats they actually want. Six takes on that moment.

Pricing used in each card: 1–4 seats $20/seat, 5–9 seats $18/seat, 10+ seats $17/seat (all billed annually). The total & Buy-now button update live as you change the input. All variations share the same three-chip row; only the space below changes.
